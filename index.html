About Me:

I am Samita Neupane and a graduate student pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science at University of South Dakota, SD, USA, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Tribhuwan University..

With over six years of experience in the software engineering industry, I have honed my skills in a wide range of technologies including Python, PHP, C, C#, JavaScript, TensorFlow, Keras, Git, and Selenium. My background spans both development and automation, allowing me to work across the full software lifecycle. My research interests center on applying AI and ML to real-world problems in healthcare and beyond. I have recently worked on projects involving neural signal decoding using EEG data and reinforcement learning for biomedical named entity recognition. I am particularly passionate about ethical AI and the use of intelligent systems to enhance healthcare solutions and optimize business operations such as sales.

Outside of tech, I love traveling, writing stories and articles, and expressing myself through dance. These passions fuel my creativity and inspire me to approach challenges with both logic and imagination.
